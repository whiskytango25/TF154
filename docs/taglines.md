Book One of six in the Task Force 154: The Reapers series.

## POSITIONAL COPY — WHAT THIS BOOK IS NOT

*For use in author interviews, press copy, and positioning against genre.*

This is not a war story.  
This is not a thriller about a rogue operator.  
This is not a patriotic military fiction.  

It is a story about the distance between what institutions say they are and what they do, and about the people who operate in that distance and have to decide, every day, what they are going to be.

The enemy is not a person. The enemy is the mechanism.
