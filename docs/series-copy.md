# TF 154 — Series Description (KDP / IngramSpark / D2D)
## Approved May 17, 2026

TF 154 is the unit that does not exist.

Born in the shadows after 9/11, Task Force 154 operates beyond command structure, public accountability, and conventional rules of engagement. Its missions are never acknowledged. Its targets are never captured. Its operators are never supposed to be remembered.

At the center is Master Chief Petty Officer Caden "Ghost" Rourke — a Tier 1 operator assigned to a deniable kill team answering to a single NSC handler. From the opening days of the Iraq War through the expanding chaos of the Global War on Terror, Rourke's team is sent where policy fails and violence becomes the only language left.

But every off-the-books mission carries a cost. And the enemy is not always across the wire.

Built for readers who want hard-edged special operations fiction, covert action, political betrayal, and morally complicated warriors operating in the gray space between duty and damnation.

No medals. No witnesses. No record.
Only the mission.
